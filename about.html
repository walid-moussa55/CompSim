About the Project

The Computer Simulator project is designed to help students and enthusiasts understand the fundamentals of computer architecture, programming, and assembly-like languages through interactive simulations and documentation.

Meet the Creator

This project was developed by Walid Ait Moussa, a passionate software engineer and educator with a deep interest in computer systems, programming languages, and educational tools. With a background in WAM development, the aim was to create an accessible and engaging platform to demystify how computers work under the hood.
